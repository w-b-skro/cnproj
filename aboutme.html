About Me



Who am I?

I'm a 26 year old man who's lived in Australia, the UK and the Netherlands. I studied History (BA) and Gender Studies (MA). I have had many jobs completely unrelated to these subjects, as well as to computers, such as candle salesman, receptionist in student housing, and as a waiter in a Peruvian restaurant. (For the last one I got to wear a traditional embroidered waistcoat each evening!)

However, the jobs I enjoyed more were ones I fell into and ended up doing IT type tasks, usually because no one else wanted to. For instance, I spent a few months working in a GP's preparing it for an inspection and ended up learning how to use databases in order to fix problems with patient records (i.e. test results disappearing.) I enjoyed the challenge of problem solving and this motivated me to look further into programming as a career possibility.

What's the point of this site?

As I learn more I'm hoping to update this site with more examples, as well as to update the site itself. As I'm still very much a beginner, I'm not hoping to wow employers or show off my skills to the internet. Rather, I want to create a record of what I'm doing to record my progress and motivate myself, as well as provide something that might be useful to other people just starting off.

Up to this point, I have found "back-end" coding to be more enjoyable, so hopefully this website will motivate me to continue to learn more CSS and HTML as well so that one day it might look a little less clunky!
